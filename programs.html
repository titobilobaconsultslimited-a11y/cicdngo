Zero Violence Ambassadors

Training young people in schools and communities to become advocates against all forms of violence. Ambassadors lead peer education campaigns and serve as first-responders for fellow students in distress — creating a student-led network of change across 110+ schools in Education District 2.

Walk-In Talk Clinic

A safe, confidential space where young people can walk in and speak with a trained counsellor or therapist. No appointment needed. No judgement. Just support when it's needed most.

Monthly Instagram Live

Every month, CICD hosts a live conversation on Instagram with experts, survivors, and advocates. Topics cover mental health, child rights, gender-based violence, and community empowerment — reaching thousands online.

Girls & Mentors Hope Rising

A mentorship and empowerment program pairing adolescent girls with professional women mentors. Participants develop life skills, career awareness, and the confidence to claim their futures — free from violence and limitation.
